Meet Your Coach - Shani Harvey

Shani Harvey is a dedicated life coach, certified nutritionist, and Master Personal Trainer passionate about helping women transform their lives. As the co-owner of Next Level Personal Training and the founder of Girl Get Your Life, she specializes in body image, nutrition, and work-life balance.

A wife, mother of three, and grandmother of three, Shani understands the challenges women face in juggling responsibilities while prioritizing their own health and happiness. She believes every woman deserves to feel confident, strong, and fulfilled. Whether it’s overcoming body image struggles, building a balanced lifestyle, or chasing long-held dreams, Shani is ready to help women break through barriers and step into their best selves. It’s time to stop putting yourself last—Girl, Get Your Life!
